You are developing a system for an online marketplace that connects sellers and buyers for
various products. The system should model entities such as Seller, Product, Customer, and
Review. you can think of some attributes for every class. For example, a seller has a name and
a list of products. A product has a name, price, description, and category. A sellTo method
receives a customer object. A customer has a name and a list of reviews and a purchase
method receives a product object. A review has a message and rating value. You can add
additional methods if you want.

Your task is to create different types of relationships (association, aggregation, composi-
tion, dependency).
